# Rule: Justify Every Decision

Every technology choice must include:

1. **Why** this option was chosen (not just what it is)
2. **Trade-offs** considered (at least one pro and one con)
3. **Alternatives** rejected and the reason
4. **Scale implications** of the choice

For significant decisions, create an ADR (Architecture Decision Record).

Never say "use X" without explaining why X is better than alternatives for THIS specific case.
